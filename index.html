❓ HOW TO PLAY

Grow a single cell into an absurd mutant. Numbers go up. Each run looks different. There is no "end" — chase bigger numbers and weirder creatures.

🖱️ Click & grow

Click the organism for biomass (your currency). Drag to spin it around.

🧫 Organelles

Buy organelles — they auto-produce biomass every second. Spread across all five: dumping everything into one has diminishing returns.

🧬 Evolve & mutations

Hit Evolve to reset your run in exchange for Evolution Points (a permanent production boost) and a draft of 3 mutations. Mutations are permanent, stack together, and physically reshape your creature (eyes, spikes, tentacles, a maw…). Rarer = stronger.

🧱 The Wall (Metabolic Pressure)

Production has a soft ceiling. As you approach it, the Metabolic Pressure bar fills and your creature strains red ("about to pop"). Past the wall, more upgrades barely help — that's your cue to Speciate.

🔬 Speciate & the Genome Lab

At the wall, Speciate: your whole build is banked as a permanent Species card, you earn Genome, and you reset deeper. In the 🧬 Genome Lab you spend Genome on upgrades (raise the wall, auto-buy organelles, +draft cards) and equip Species for permanent bonuses — equipped species also layer ghostly onto your creature.

✨ Mitogen Blooms & Digest (temporary boosts!)

Now and then a golden Bloom grows on your creature — click it for a big ×4 production frenzy that lasts 20 seconds. The 🍴 Digest button spends 40% of your biomass for a similar temporary surge.

⚡ While a boost is active your /sec turns gold with a ⚡ and a countdown. When it ends, /sec drops back to normal — that's the boost wearing off, not your upgrades breaking.

🏆 Achievements & 📊 Stats

Achievements grant small permanent bonuses. Open 📊 Stats to see your collection — 50 mutations and 15 achievements to discover.

⚙️ Settings

Pick a music theme, tune screen shake (or turn it off), and toggle reduce motion.
